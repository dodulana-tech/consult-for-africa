Consult for Africa exists to close that gap. We are Africa's healthcare
transformation partner, working alongside operators and institutions to

And around that core, we have built three things African healthcare has long

**Leadership assessment and coaching for healthcare's decision-makers.**

The difference between a high-performing institution and a failing one is

**The workforce platform connecting your facility to the talent it needs.**
