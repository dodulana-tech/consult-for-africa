# Maarova Leadership Profile: Pilot Cohort Recruitment

**Prepared by:** Consult For Africa
**Date:** 30 April 2026
**Owner:** Debo Odulana
**Status:** Pilot recruitment playbook v1.0

---

## 1. Goal

Recruit and complete **50 to 75 healthcare leaders** through the Maarova assessment between now and 30 June 2026. Outcomes:

- Generate enough completed assessments to calculate Cronbach's alpha and validate reliability per module (target greater than 0.70).
- Surface qualitative feedback on report value, language, and credibility from real users.
- Build an inaugural cohort whose shared profiles and case studies seed Phase 1 marketing.

This is a credential-validation pilot, not a commercial launch. Pilot participants get the assessment, full report, and shareable profile at no cost in exchange for feedback.

---

## 2. Target Audience

Three concentric rings, prioritised by warmth.

### Ring 1: Warm network (target: 20 participants)

People in Debo's and the wider C4A network who already know the Maarova story. Highest yield, lowest friction.

- Existing C4A consultants who have not yet taken the assessment
- Cooked Indoors, Covally, Connexxum, Cureva client-side leaders
- HealthStack Africa contacts and EMR advocacy network
- Personal contacts among medical directors, hospital CEOs, board chairs

### Ring 2: Active outreach (target: 25 to 35 participants)

Targeted via the existing OutreachCampaign system in the platform.

- Medical directors and CEOs of mid-sized private hospitals in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt
- Nursing directors at the same facilities
- Faculty of healthcare leadership at LBS, PAU, Wits, Stellenbosch
- WHO AFRO and MSH leadership programme alumni who can be identified
- MANSAG and ANPA diaspora members who lead clinical departments

### Ring 3: Channel partnerships (target: 5 to 15 participants)

Quick wins where one introduction unlocks several leaders.

- One state hospital CEO forum (e.g., Lagos State Health Service Commission CEOs)
- One private hospital group HR head agreeing to invite their leadership team
- One academic department head sponsoring a faculty cohort

## 3. The Offer

> Take the Maarova leadership assessment, free of charge. Get your personalised leadership profile and a verified credential you can attach to your CV. We are calibrating the assessment for African healthcare and want a small, founding cohort of leaders to shape the final product.

**What participants get:**

- Full leadership assessment (about 60 minutes, online, mobile-friendly)
- Personalised report with archetype, signature strengths, development edges
- Single-page Maarova Leadership Profile PDF for the CV
- Public verification URL they can share or revoke at any time
- Optional 30-minute coaching debrief with a C4A senior consultant
- Founding-cohort recognition in Phase 1 case studies (with consent)

**What we ask in return:**

- Complete the assessment within 21 days of the invitation
- Five-minute structured feedback survey after completing the report
- Permission to use anonymised score data for psychometric validation
- Optional: agree to be a quoted case study (separate consent)

## 6. Operational Setup

Use the existing OutreachCampaign system in the platform.

- Create one campaign per ring (`Pilot Cohort Q2 2026 - Ring 1`, `Ring 2`, `Ring 3`).
- Add targets via bulk import for Ring 2 and Ring 3, one-by-one for Ring 1.
- Each target gets a unique invite token (already implemented).
- Token expires after 30 days; extend manually for any leader who asks.

After completion, the leader appears in the existing Maarova portal with a session, report, and a Share section that lets them enable a public profile and download the CV PDF.

## 7. Feedback Survey (post-completion)

Five questions, sent immediately after report generation. Should take under five minutes.

1. Did the assessment cover the things that matter for healthcare leadership in your context? (Yes, mostly, partially, not really)
2. Did the report describe you accurately enough to be useful? (Very accurate, mostly, somewhat, not really)
3. Is there anything in the report you found surprising, helpful, or off?
4. Would you put the Maarova credential on your CV or LinkedIn? Why or why not?
5. Who else in your network would benefit? Names and contacts welcome.

## 8. Success Criteria

- 50 to 75 completed assessments by 30 June 2026
- At least 30 with all six modules complete (including 360 where opted in)
- Cronbach's alpha greater than 0.70 across DISC, EQ, CILTI, Culture, Values
- At least 30 feedback surveys returned
- At least 10 leaders agreeing to be quoted case studies
- At least 25 leaders enabling their public profile and sharing it externally

---

## 9. Tracking

Maintain a simple campaign dashboard in Notion or Google Sheets with one row per target:

- Name, role, organisation, ring, source
- Date invited, date started, date completed
- Did they enable sharing? Did they download CV PDF?
- Feedback survey sent, returned, NPS-style summary
- Case-study consent yes or no
- Notes on any issues or quotes worth keeping

Review weekly. Adjust outreach pace and targeting based on actual response rates.

---

## 10. Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Slow response from cold targets | Lean harder on Ring 1 and partner-led for first 30 leaders; treat Ring 2 as longer-tail |
| Reliability metric below 0.70 on a module | Flag the weak module; tighten the question bank before public launch; do not credential against that module's score |
| Leaders complete assessment but find report shallow | Treat as the most valuable feedback; iterate the report prompts before Phase 1 |
| Privacy concerns about scores | Publish a clear privacy note; emphasise that public profile shows only marketing-safe content; revocation is one click |
| Negative reactions to one's own profile | Offer the optional 30-minute debrief with a senior consultant; do not publish individual scores anywhere |
