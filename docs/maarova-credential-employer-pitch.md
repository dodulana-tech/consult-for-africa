## 1. The Pitch in One Paragraph

Maarova is the first verified leadership credential designed specifically for African healthcare. Hospitals and partners can recognise it the way they recognise FACHE in the United States: as objective, validated evidence that a leader has been assessed against a rigorous competency framework. We are inviting a small group of founding partners to formally acknowledge the credential in their hiring, promotion, or development decisions, and in return, to receive privileged early access for their own leadership cohorts.

---

## 2. Why a Credential, and Why Now

Healthcare leadership in Africa is selected on three signals: clinical credentials, seniority, and personal networks. None of those measure leadership capability directly. The result is well documented: senior clinicians promoted into administrative roles they were not assessed for, succession planning driven by tenure, and boards relying on intuition rather than evidence.

International tools (Hogan, SHL, DISC) exist but were not built for African healthcare contexts. Western normative data, language, and case scenarios reduce their credibility with African boards. Local academic research is fragmented and not commercially available.

Maarova fills the gap. It is psychometrically grounded, contextualised to African healthcare leadership, and delivered as a single, verifiable credential with clear language a board chair can read.

---

## 3. What the Credential Is

A Maarova Leadership Profile is awarded to a leader who completes the full assessment. It includes:

- A verified public profile page at a permanent URL
- A single-page PDF designed to attach to a CV
- A leadership archetype that summarises the leader at a glance
- Top dimensions across DISC, values, emotional intelligence, the clinical-to-leadership transition, and team culture
- A verification footer linking back to the official Maarova profile

The leader controls visibility. The full report and development areas remain private.

## 5. The Value Exchange

This is a structured trade, not a request for endorsement.

**What the partner gives:**

- Time of one decision-maker for an introductory call (30 minutes)
- Public agreement to recognise the credential in a defined process
- An opening to invite a defined cohort of leaders into the assessment

**What the partner gets:**

- Free or significantly discounted assessment access for an inaugural cohort
- A co-marketing slot in the public launch (Q4 2026)
- Quarterly aggregated insights on the leadership profile of their cohort, anonymised
- A seat on the Maarova advisory circle (informal, two virtual meetings per year)
- Early access to the Phase 2 features that matter to them (cohort dashboards, normative benchmarking, succession-planning views)

## 7. Anticipated Questions

**Is Maarova validated?**

> Yes. The instrument was built on Hofstede, GLOBE, NHS CLCF, and ACHE frameworks, contextualised for African healthcare. We are completing reliability validation with a founding cohort of 50 to 75 leaders this quarter. Initial Cronbach's alpha targets are 0.70 or higher per module.

**How is this different from Hogan or DISC?**

> Maarova combines psychometric assessment with a healthcare-specific module called CILTI, the Clinical Identity to Leadership Identity Transition. No off-the-shelf international instrument has this. Our normative data is being built from African healthcare leaders, not Western corporate samples.

**What if a leader scores poorly?**

> The full report is private to the leader. Only the leader chooses what to share publicly. The shared profile is marketing-safe: archetype, signature strengths, and top dimensions only. Coaching priorities and development edges remain confidential.

**Does the credential expire?**

> Yes. Three-year cycle, in line with FACHE, ICF, and PMP. Leaders renew either by retaking the assessment or by accumulating continuing-education credits through Maarova coaching programmes.

**What does a partnership cost?**

> For founding partners in the categories above, nothing in Year 1 beyond a defined cohort commitment. Standard commercial pricing applies from Year 2 unless we structure a longer-term arrangement.

**Who issues the credential?**

> Consult For Africa, through the Maarova platform. The verification page is at maarova.com/profile and shows the issuer, the leader's archetype, signature strengths, top dimensions, and the date of issue. Anyone can verify a profile by visiting the URL.

**What happens to my cohort's data?**

> Individual reports stay with the leader. Aggregated, anonymised cohort data can be shared with the partner under a separate agreement. We do not sell or share individual data with third parties.
